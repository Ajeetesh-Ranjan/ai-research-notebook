# CiteMind — Feature Overview

> **CiteMind** is an AI-powered research notebook that reads, understands, connects, and exports your documents — turning scattered PDFs into structured knowledge.

---

## PDF & Document Intelligence

- Upload and read PDF, DOCX, TXT, and Markdown files side-by-side.
- High-fidelity PDF viewer with smooth scroll, zoom, and page navigation.
- Highlight text in any colour and attach contextual notes instantly.
- Extract key quotes, figures, and tables with one click.
- Auto-detect document structure (abstract, sections, references, tables).
- Full-text search across every uploaded document in milliseconds.

## AI Research Assistant

- Ask natural-language questions about any document and get cited answers.
- Auto-summarize papers, reports, and chapters with source-linked bullets.
- Multi-document reasoning: synthesize insights across 50+ papers at once.
- Semantic search: find related concepts even when keywords differ.
- Auto-generate literature-review paragraphs with inline citations.
- Detect contradictions and agreements across multiple sources.

## Visual Knowledge Mapping

- Interactive knowledge graph auto-built from document entities and themes.
- Drag, zoom, and explore connections between papers, concepts, and authors.
- Colour-coded node types: treaties, mechanisms, targets, finance, methods.
- One-click jump from any graph node to its source document and highlight.
- Export graphs as PNG, SVG, or embed into reports.

## Research Board & Workflow

- Kanban-style research board: To Read → Reading → Reviewed → Cited.
- Drag papers between columns to track research progress visually.
- AI suggestions for grouping related papers and finding gaps.
- Tag system with custom labels, priorities, and due dates.
- Research templates for systematic reviews, meta-analyses, and policy briefs.

## Citations & Export

- One-click citation generation in APA, MLA, Chicago, Harvard, and BibTeX.
- Export to Word (.docx) with formatted citations and bibliography.
- Export to PowerPoint (.pptx) with slides from highlights and boards.
- Export to Markdown (.md), PDF report, or interactive HTML.
- Sync directly to Notion databases with preserved structure.
- All exports include back-links to source documents and page numbers.

## Collaboration & Sharing

- Share projects with team members via invite links.
- Real-time comment threads on highlights and annotations.
- Version history for every document and note.
- Team-wide knowledge graphs that merge individual research.
- Admin dashboard for user management, storage, and billing.

## Security & Enterprise

- AES-256 encryption at rest; TLS 1.3 in transit.
- SSO via Google, Microsoft, and SAML 2.0.
- Role-based access control: Owner, Editor, Viewer.
- Tenant isolation with dedicated vector namespaces.
- Audit logs for every document access, AI query, and export.
- GDPR, SOC 2 Type II, and HIPAA compliance ready.
